The Secret Life of Cats and Dogs

This is about how cats and dogs act when the owners are gone.

How well do you know your family members?

They are important members of the family, but how well do we really know them? Take an intimate and informative look at the world of our closest canine and feline friends, and find out how dogs and cats change from blind and deaf newborns into highly sophisticated animals.

Super senses?

How do they grow to have super senses, with the power to enhance and sometimes even save our lives? As you follow a puppy from birth to motherhood and a new-born kitten to nimble adulthood, you will understand our pets extraordinary behavior as never before, and love them more than ever.
